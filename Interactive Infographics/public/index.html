Ways To Prevent Air Pollution

Use air masks to keep out dust, pollutants, bacteria and viruses.

Keep indoor plants that help purify the air.

Use air purifiers to help everyone breathe and sleep better.

Try to club activities together to avoid multiple trips to the city.

Stay indoors as far as possible.

Don't burn garbage use sustainable waste management.

Drink plenty of fluids to flush out toxins.

Eat the right foods that help to boost our immune systems.

Use heavy curtains and keep the polluted air from entering your homes.
